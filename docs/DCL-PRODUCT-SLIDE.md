# DCL Engine
## Unify Your Data. Visualize Your Enterprise.

---

### The Challenge

Enterprise data lives in **11+ disconnected systems** — CRMs, ERPs, databases, warehouses. Each team sees a fragment. Nobody sees the whole picture.

---

### The Solution: DCL Engine

**One semantic layer. One visual truth.**

DCL automatically discovers, maps, and visualizes how your data flows from source systems to business outcomes — in real time.

### Intelligent Mapping

DCL learns from every mapping decision:

| Low Confidence? | AI Validates |
|-----------------|--------------|
| `GL_ACCOUNT` → "account"? | **Corrected:** → "general_ledger" |
| `MRR` → "revenue"? | **Confirmed:** ✓ |

*Continuous learning improves accuracy over time.*

---

### Zero-Trust Architecture

DCL never stores your data. Ever.

| What DCL Stores | What DCL Does NOT Store |
|-----------------|------------------------|
| Schema metadata (field names, types) | Row data |
| Mapping decisions | Customer records |
| Pointers to data locations | Actual payloads |

**Your data stays in your systems. DCL only maps the structure.**
